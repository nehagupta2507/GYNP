About US

Global Youth Network for Peace (GYNP) is a global membership association of peacebuilding organizations, professionals and a network of people developing processes for change in the most complex, chaotic conflict environments in Sierra Leone and around the world.

We are a peacebuilding Nonprofit-Non-Governmental Organization working with and supporting people at the heart of conflicts who are striving to find solutions to peace and security related issues. We work with them to deepen our collective understanding of the conflict, bring together divided communities and create opportunities for them to resolve their differences peacefully.


Mission

Global Youth Network for Peace seeks to adopt democratic principles and inclusive solution-based approach in preventing, mitigating and resolving violent conflicts and build a world of hope for everyone, everywhere.

Vision

Global Youth Network for Peace's vision is to create a peaceful and developed world for everyone, everywhere!
